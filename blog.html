About Me

Workspace is our playful ode to office horseplay (minus the inevitable eye loss). It features loads of animated detail and the cutest pile of interactive desk crap this side of a Lisa Frank store. A pen, coffee mug, glasses and more are all moveable, complemented by cleverly cartoonish post containers. Pick it up today for $49.
